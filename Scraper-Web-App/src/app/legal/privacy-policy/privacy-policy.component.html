What are cookies?

Cookies are packets of data which your device downloads each time you visit a website. When you return to websites, or visit other websites that use the same cookie, the websites or associated vendors recognize your cookies and therefore your device.

What cookies does InsiderScraper use?

InsiderScraper uses third-party cookies. These types of cookies are placed on your device from outside of the website you are currently one.
